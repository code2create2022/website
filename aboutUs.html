Code To Create

Code To Create is a JBCN Borivali learner led initiative by the members of the Programming Club. It aims to provide a platform for all the young minds to ideate, innovate and incorporate projects in an impactful manner.

It is backed by few industry specialists and hopes that the participants of the competition leave with tremendous and experiential learnings. We hope to welcome your participation in the second edition of Code To Create.

Registration for the event is free of cost. Participants will compete in teams of 2 to 4 members being part of the age group of 13 to 18 years old.
